FAQ

How many revision rounds are included?

Basic includes 2 rounds. Standard and Premium include 3. Extra rounds are billed hourly ($40/hr).

What do you need from me?

Logo/brand, copy points, images (or stock preferences), and any integration logins. See our onboarding checklist on the Process page.

Do you migrate content from my old site?

Yes. Simple pages are included in scope; complex CMS migrations are quoted as an add‑on.

Can you handle domains & DNS?

Yes — domain is $60/yr including DNS management and connection to the site.

Do I own my website?

Absolutely. After final payment, you own the code and content. We can host/maintain it or hand it off.

How do payments work?

50% deposit to start; 50% on pre‑launch preview. Card/bank accepted. Invoices provided.
