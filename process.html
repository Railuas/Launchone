Process & Timeline

  1. 1Discovery (1–2 days) — Goals, audience, references, scope, and content checklist.
  2. 2Design (2–5 days) — Wireframe & hero concepts, then full page comps.
  3. 3Build (3–7 days) — Semantic code, motion, forms, integrations.
  4. 4QA & SEO (1–2 days) — Cross‑device testing, CWV, metadata, OG.
  5. 5Launch (same day) — Netlify deploy, DNS, analytics, backups.
  6. 6Handover — Loom walkthrough + docs. Ongoing care via a plan.

Onboarding Checklist

  • Brand assets (logo, colors, fonts)
  • Copy or bullet points for each section
  • High‑res images or stock preferences
  • Social links, contact details
  • Any integrations (calendar, payments, CRM)

Launch Checklist

  • Meta titles/descriptions + OG images
  • Sitemap + robots, 404/redirects
  • Core Web Vitals pass
  • Analytics + consent
  • Backups, SSL, uptime monitor
